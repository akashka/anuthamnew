About Us

Anutham is a sanskrit word which means ‘The Finest’. Anutham's core team is constantly working on being a better version of themselves and hence the name - ANUTHAM. We believe in learning and growing as we move forward. With this philosophy, we wish to provide the ‘finest’ of services to our customers during our special journey called "LIFE".

Anutham is an amalgamation of entrepreneurs (mainly women) who come together on one platform to showcase their products / services. As our first step, we are starting out with some vibrant and high-energy entrepreneurs who bring to you products in Apparels, Accessories, Interior Design and Learning Solutions (skill and behavioral).

As we move forward in this journey, we will add more categories like Food, Photography, and continuously strive to bring you the best.

Our Three Verticals

ANUTHAM

Anutham showcase Handloom Sarees, Silks of KancheePuram, Punjabi Designer Dress Material, Punjabi Jutti's, Himachali Shawls & Accesories.

MNTR

MNTR take care of Skill & Behavioural based Training modules for Corporates & Open Workshops.

NEEV

NEEV take care of Interior Designing of Commercial Office Spaces, Retail Outlets, Residence and Commercial Buildings including Wallpappers, Vinyl Flooring


Mission

Anutham - Endeavors to be "Committed - Transparent & Professional" in offering the finest services in all the Verticals. At Anutham, we are Smart, Sensitive and complete believer in working together and growing along with the community, customer and our partners.


Vision

At Anutham, we celebrate Happiness & focus on becoming the best platform to support various artisans, weavers, women entreprenuers of India and take their products from India to the world. And together envision a future that is Bolder, Beautiful & finest for all.
